🐾 Welcome to “Cat vs Mouse Rain” – The Ultimate Reflex Challenge!

Get ready for a fast-paced, heart-pounding, and wildly entertaining arcade game where your only mission is survival! In Cat vs Mouse Rain, you play as a cute, cartoon-style cat on a road with mountains and clouds in the background. Your job? Dodge a relentless shower of falling mice while staying firmly on the path. The more mice you escape, the higher your score – but don’t blink, because the pace keeps getting faster!

🎮 Simple Gameplay, Endless Fun

Move the cat left or right with simple controls and avoid the falling mice. Each second you survive and every mouse you dodge adds to your high score. It’s easy to play, but hard to master – perfect for casual gamers and challenge lovers alike.

  • Watch your score increase the longer you survive
  • Try again and beat your high score!
